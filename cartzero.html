03 · Technology Stack
Core CPMS
AMPECO (AWS)
OCPP-based Charge Point Management System. ToU-optimized overnight charging. Predictive swap scheduling. Remote fleet monitoring. Founder deploys this professionally today at 25,000 charge points.
Battery IoT Layer
Smart BMS + LTE-M
Smart BMS with integrated IoT gateway. OCPP communication to AMPECO. 1NCE SIM cards (500MB lifetime, ~$10/SIM). Real-time SoC, temperature, and cell voltage telemetry.

ESCO + REC Retirement
100% renewable electricity via certified ESCO. REC retirement through NYGATS. Genuine zero-carbon claim — verified, not greenwashing. +$0.01/kWh vs standard rate.
