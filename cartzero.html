The Commissary Margin Model

NYC law mandates every food cart vendor visits a licensed commissary every single day. Batteries travel with vendors to this mandatory touchpoint. CartZero does not build a delivery fleet — we integrate into existing compliance infrastructure that already moves vendors through our hubs every 24 hours. Gross margin 50%+ from day one.

This is not just regulatory — it is contractual. Every vendor signs a DOHMH Commissary Agreement committing to daily visits, punishable as a Class A Misdemeanor if violated. CartZero's distribution channel is written into a legal contract every vendor in our market has personally signed.

Automatic DOHMH Compliance
Logs. Zero Manual Work.
DOHMH requires commissaries to maintain daily use logs per vendor — date, time, decal number. CartZero's AMPECO CPMS generates this log automatically as a byproduct of every battery swap. Commissary operators get regulatory compliance for free.
